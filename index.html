If you have been separated from someone in a crowd, post a notice here. Anyone can read the board and reply. Replies are locked to the person who posted — nobody else, including whoever runs this board, can read them.

Enter the counterfoil code you were given when you posted. It is the only key that unlocks your replies — keep it, we cannot recover it.

What this board stores. The description you type is public — anyone can read it. Replies are encrypted in your browser to a key only your counterfoil can open. No account, no phone number and no device ID is recorded with a notice. To stop flooding we keep a 60-second counter under a one-way, daily-rotating hash of your IP address \u2014 never the address itself.

What to write. Enough to recognise a person — approximate age, clothing, where and when they were last seen. Nothing more. Do not post ID card numbers, addresses, vehicle numbers, or a photograph. A notice that identifies someone too precisely can be used against them later.

Auto-delete. Notices and replies are erased 72 hours after posting. You can delete yours sooner with your counterfoil.

This is not an emergency service. For a medical emergency call 102, police 112, fire 101. If a child is missing, contact the nearest police officer or control room immediately — do not wait on this board.
